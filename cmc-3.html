Vision of the Future

CMC 3 is a medical mall developed by NBIG Developments. The company, founded over 13 years ago by engineer Mohamed Abdel Moneim Ammar, specializes in designing and executing residential, commercial, and administrative projects in both the Egyptian and Emirati markets.
