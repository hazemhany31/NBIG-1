Strategic Location

6th of October City

CMC is strategically positioned in Egypt's 6th of October City. The project is located behind Mall of Arabia and on Gamal Abdel Nasser axis. It is situated on three main axes, surrounded by more than 35 residential compounds. This prime location offers:

  • Close proximity to major clubs like El Seid Club and October Club.
  • Near top universities such as Misr University for Science and Technology and October University for Modern Sciences and Arts.
  • Connected to Cairo via major highways
  • High patient accessibility and demand
  • Close to major hospitals including 6th of October University Hospital and Misr International Hospital.
