Project Overview

Sky-Hills Compound was established in collaboration between Dana Real Estate Development (Owner), New Build International Group (NBIG) (Developer), Dorra Contracting (General Contractor), and Moharram-Bakhoum (Owner’s Consultant).

The project provides a modern and integrated lifestyle with affordable prices and a variety of unit sizes in a strategic location.

Our vision is to enable future generations to enjoy a healthy, peaceful, and smart way of living.
