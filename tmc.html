Project Overview

Tiba Med Center (TMC) is the first integrated medical mall in the area, combining specialized medical clinics and diverse commercial spaces. Developed by NBIG Developments, with over 13 years of experience in residential, commercial, and administrative projects in Egypt and the UAE. The project aims to meet the needs of doctors, investors, patients, and visitors.

  • Strategic location and modern design.
  • Unique experience combining healthcare and shopping.
  • Includes 50 medical clinics, a pharmacy, and 2 labs.
  • Features a radiology center, mini market, and café.

Strategic Location

6th of October City

Tiba Med Center is located in 6th of October City, behind Mall of Arabia and Gamal Abdel Nasser Axis. The project is in the clubs and palaces area, steps away from the tourist walkway and Gamal Abdel Nasser Axis. It is next to the largest residential complexes and surrounded by more than 35 compounds.

  • Near Mall of Arabia, 6th of October Club, and Shooting Club.
  • Close to Boulevard Axis and July 26 Axis.
  • Near universities and schools like MUST.
  • Location ensures easy access and high demand for services.
