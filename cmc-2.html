Project Overview

CMC 2 is a state-of-the-art medical mall developed by NBIG Developments, an extension of the successful CMC 1. The project provides a modern, professional, and integrated environment for healthcare professionals and patients.

  • Strategic location on Ahmed Zewail Road in Hadayek October.
  • Directly opposite the City Authority and Petroleum Company.
  • 90 fully-finished and air-conditioned clinics.
  • Advanced medical facilities including radiology and labs.

Features & Services

Designed to meet modern medical needs with state-of-the-art features and reliable support, CMC 2 ensures a high-quality experience for all.

Smart Technology & Mobile App

An integrated smart system with a mobile app for doctors and patients to manage appointments and medical records.

Comprehensive Medical Facilities

Includes an on-site pharmacy, radiology centers, and two medical analysis labs.

Central Reception

A central reception desk on every floor to assist visitors and manage clinic services.

24/7 Security & Sterilization

Controlled access, security systems, and periodic sterilization for a safe environment.

Central Wi-Fi

Reliable and high-speed Wi-Fi access throughout the mall for all users.

Parking & Access

Ample parking areas, six elevators, and six staircases for ease of access and convenience.

Strategic Location

Hadayek October

CMC 2 is strategically positioned in the heart of Hadayek October, offering high accessibility and a promising investment opportunity. The project is located on Ahmed Zewail Road and opposite the City Authority.

  • Directly in front of the October Gardens City Authority and the Petroleum Company.
  • Close to major residential compounds and main roads.
  • High patient demand due to the population density in the area.
  • Quick access to medical services and labs.
  • Ideal for all medical specialties seeking a prime location.
